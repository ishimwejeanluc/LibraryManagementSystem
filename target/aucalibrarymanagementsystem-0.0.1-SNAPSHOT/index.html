About Us

The AUCA Library System is designed to enhance the library management experience by streamlining book borrowing, membership tracking, and fee management. Our mission is to support learning and research through a well-organized system and accessible resources.

Our Services

Book Borrowing

Borrow books based on your membership type, with flexible return dates and minimal hassle.

Shelving System

Easy-to-use shelving and room management to keep the library organized and accessible.

Late Fees

Automated late fee calculation to ensure fair usage of library resources for everyone.
